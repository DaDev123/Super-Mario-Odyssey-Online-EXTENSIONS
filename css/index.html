Super Luigi Odyssey

Super Luigi Odyssey is an unofficial mod created by Wexos and Atlas which makes Luigi playable as the main character in Super Mario Odyssey. All icons, outfits, text, layout and more have been edited to make the game Luigi-themed. Even though both authors have spent hundreds of hours on this mod, it is still not perfect.

Explore the same kingdoms as in Super Mario Odyssey, and help the green-eyed Cappy to save his beloved sister Tiara, and Peach who was kidnapped by Bowser. Collect Power Moons, which is the fuel for the Odyssey, and travel between the different kingdoms and explore the world.
Fight the four Broodals: Spewart, Topper, Rango, and Hariet, and collect Multi Moons which are worth as much as 3 Power Moons. Join this epic journey and at the end, fight against Bowser himself to stop his wedding with Peach, in an epic final fight on the Moon.
Connect online to play Mario's Balloon World. In this game you search for balloons, hidden by other players in the different kingdoms. When you find a balloon, you get coins as a reward. More difficult balloons mean more coins. You can also hide your own balloons and let other people find them. A great way of earning coins, while having fun at the same time.
