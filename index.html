Super Mario Odyssey Multiplayer Extensions

Super Mario Odyssey Multiplayer is a mod created by Craftyboss and Sanae which makes Mario Odyssey playable in Multiplayer in the same World, this mod supports Custom Gamemodes such as: Hide and Seek, Freeze Tag...
Super Mario Odyssey Multiplayer is a free fan-made project.
